About IERI

IERI (Institute for Ecological Research and Innovation) is a pioneering architectural firm at the forefront of sustainable and innovative design. Founded by visionary architect Gocha Mindilaia, IERI is dedicated to creating spaces that harmoniously blend with their environment while pushing the boundaries of modern architecture.

Our team of skilled architects, engineers, and designers brings a wealth of experience and a passion for eco-friendly solutions to every project. At IERI, we believe that great architecture should not only be aesthetically pleasing but also environmentally responsible and socially impactful.

From residential homes that embrace natural surroundings to cutting-edge commercial spaces that prioritize energy efficiency, IERI's portfolio showcases a diverse range of projects. Each design is a testament to our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and the creation of spaces that inspire and endure.

At the heart of IERI's philosophy is the belief that architecture has the power to shape a better future. We continuously explore new technologies, materials, and design methodologies to create buildings that are not only beautiful but also contribute positively to the environment and the communities they serve.
